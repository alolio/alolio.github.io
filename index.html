Our story

  • February 2000

    ICQ

    Alona connects to ICQ and selects random chat. Looking for an online penpal to practice English she finds Lionel. We spends long hours chatting and forming a very personal connection.

  • August 2000

    We first meet in Ukraine

    After 6 months of online communications, we first meet in person in Ukraine in August 2000. For two weeks, we have fabulous vacations. It is love!

  • 2001

    Ukraine Trips

    We keep a long distance relationship online. We spend a lot of time chatting and sending emails to each other. Lionel visits Alona twice a year in Ukraine, for Summer and Winter vacations.

  • 2002

    Alona moves to Toronto

    Alona and her family moves to Toronto in Winter 2002. Lionel visits them in Spring and Summer.

  • 2002

    Alona visits France

    Alona visits France for the first time and meets Lionel's family.

  • 2003-2004

    Lionel moves to Montreal

    Lionel moves to Montreal to study. We are now upgraded to a medium distance relationship. We travel between Montreal and Toronto to visit each other frequently on week-ends and holidays.

  • 2008

    Living together

    Alona moves to Montreal to study and moves in with Lionel.

  • 2014

    Engaged!

    After studies and many years together, we are now engaged!
